# Restaurant Letter of Intent (LOI) — sharmeats MVP Cohort

**This is not a binding contract. It is a mutual statement of intent to work together on the closed-beta launch of sharmeats in Sharm El Sheikh.** The legally binding commercial agreement will be signed before the restaurant goes live on the platform.

The LOI exists because:
- You (the restaurant) want to know what we're offering before committing time to onboarding (menu photos, tablet training, prep-time tuning).
- We (sharmeats) need a paper signal that you'll actually go live, so we can prioritize engineering and rider supply against your zone.

---

## What We're Building

A food delivery app for Sharm El Sheikh, focused first on the tourist market (European, Russian, Egyptian guests at Naama Bay hotels, resorts, apartments, and homes).

What makes us different from Talabat / Twista / Bringit:
- App available in **5 languages** at launch: English, Arabic, Russian, Italian, German.
- **Hotel-room delivery as a first-class feature** — guests pick their hotel and room number directly. No need for a local phone or Egyptian address.
- **International payments** — guests can pay with their home-country card, and see prices in EUR / USD / GBP / RUB.
- **15-minute delivery SLA backed by automatic credits** — if delivery is late, the customer gets compensated without calling support. This protects your rating from issues outside your kitchen.
- **Real-time order tracking** with rider photo + plate number for guest trust.
- Curated, hand-picked restaurants — not a long-tail of 500. Tourist-safe badge for restaurants that meet a clear quality bar.

## What We're Offering the First 20 Restaurants (the "Founding Cohort")

### Commercial terms (closed beta, first 3 months from your go-live date)

| Item | Founding cohort | Standard (after month 3) |
|---|---|---|
| Commission per order | **12%** (vs Talabat 22–28%, Twista 18–22%) | 18% |
| Setup fee | EGP 0 | EGP 500 |
| Tablet / hardware | We provide free | Restaurant-owned or rented EGP 200/mo |
| Menu photography | We send a photographer once, free | EGP 1,500 one-time |
| Account management | Dedicated WhatsApp line with our Sharm ops lead | Shared support queue |
| Min order guarantee | **If you receive fewer than 20 orders in month 1, we credit you EGP 1,000** | None |
| Payment terms | Weekly payout (every Sunday), bank transfer | Same |
| Exclusivity | **None.** Keep listing on Talabat, Twista, Bringit, Elmenus. We compete on results. | Same |

### Non-commercial commitments from us

- Your menu listing will be live within 7 days of LOI signature (subject to your menu photos + prices being delivered to us).
- Your restaurant will be eligible for the "Tourist-Safe" badge if you meet the simple criteria (English menu, allergen flags, accurate operating hours).
- We will deliver at least one promotional push to our waitlist when you go live (one email + one Instagram post).
- We will not give your name to anyone — not press, not other restaurants — without your written permission, until the public launch.

### What we ask from you

- A signed copy of this LOI (one page).
- A 30-minute kitchen visit from our ops lead to verify operating hours, pickup logistics, prep time.
- Your full menu with prices in EGP, in either Arabic or English (we translate).
- Permission for our photographer to spend 90 minutes in your restaurant photographing top dishes.
- A nominated point-of-contact (name + WhatsApp number) for order issues.
- A good-faith commitment to accept orders during your stated operating hours during the closed beta (we understand mistakes happen; we ask that you tell us if you need to pause).

## What Happens Next

1. You sign this LOI (digital signature or physical, either works).
2. Within 14 days: sharmeats ops lead visits your kitchen, photographs menu, sets up your tablet/WhatsApp flow.
3. Within 21 days: you go live in the closed beta.
4. Month 1–3: you operate at 12% commission. We collect feedback.
5. Before month 4: we present the standard commercial agreement. You can renew, renegotiate, or leave.

If at any point in the first 90 days you want out, you can leave with 7 days' written notice. No penalty. We will not chase you. We do ask for a 15-minute exit conversation so we can learn.
